south indian cuisine includes the cuisines of the five southern states of India. They are typically vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes for all five states.

North cuisine is a part of cuisine, from the region of Northern India which include cuisines of Jammu and Kashmir, Punjab, Chandigarh, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Rajasthan, Uttarakhand, Delhi, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Jharkhand, Madhya Pradesh and Chhattisgarh.

Seafood is any form of sea life regarded as food by humans, prominently including fish and shellfish. Shellfish include various species of molluscs (e.g. bivalve molluscs such as clams, oysters, and mussels and cephalopods such as octopus and squid)
